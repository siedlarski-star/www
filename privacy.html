Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

LingoBar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

Local Storage

Favorite words and app preferences (such as display interval, selected category, and language) are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. LingoBar does not process or store any payment information.

Third Parties

LingoBar does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
